About Me

Hello! My name is Pica Nievas and I am from Austin Texas. I enjoy playing board games, making cakes and being with my family. Some of the board games that I like to play are on the longer side usually more than two hours long. My favorite board game right now is probably Wonderland Wars, the theme of the game being Alice in Wonderland. Playing board games with my family is also a great time to gather together witch is something thing that I love to do. I also love to make cakes. I have run my own cake decorating business from my home for 8 years. My favorite types of cakes to make are wedding cakes. I love how big and dramatic they are. I really like to design with the fresh flowers included in my cakes so that is always a little different and beautiful.

Austin Texas

Texas is one of the largest states in the US. Austin is the capitol and is also known as the live music capitol. Some people might think of Texas as a dusty desert landscape and while those areas are part of Texas, Austin is an oasis of green trees, rivers and lakes.
